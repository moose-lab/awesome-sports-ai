# Awesome Sports AI: 2026 Strategic Plan & Execution Report

## Overview
As requested, I have analyzed the `awesome-sports-ai` repository, researched the hottest trends in the sports AI landscape for 2026, and integrated a comprehensive roadmap to make the project highly welcoming and relevant. The goal is to position this repository as the premier hub for developers building the next generation of sports AI tools.

## Key 2026 Sports AI Trends Identified
Based on current market research and industry projections [1] [2]:
1. **FIFA World Cup 2026 Tech**: Massive investments by FIFA and partners (like Lenovo) into AI-enabled 3D player avatars, Football AI Pro, and advanced VAR technologies.
2. **Generative AI & Multimodal LLMs**: The rise of video-reasoning models (e.g., SportR) that allow for automated commentary, highlight clipping, and tactical querying directly from video.
3. **Explosion of Women's Sports**: Surging viewership in the WNBA and women's soccer demands dedicated analytics, such as the "Gravity" metric introduced by WNBA Inside.
4. **Emerging Sports Analytics**: Grassroots and fast-growing sports like Pickleball and Esports are seeing an influx of lightweight, mobile-first computer vision coaching apps (e.g., PB Vision).

## Actions Taken

### 1. Created the 2026 Strategic Roadmap
I drafted a new document, `docs/roadmap-2026-strategy.md`, which outlines the vision and specifically proposes high-profile, early-stage open-source projects for the community to build. These "mono-tools" include:
* `llm-match-commentator`: A RAG system for live, multi-language commentary.
* `var-vision-lite`: A lightweight CV tool for offside detection.
* `wnba-gravity-mapper`: A tool to calculate player gravity from public data.
* `pickleball-court-mapper`: A mobile-friendly CV tool for amateur match analysis.

### 2. Updated the Enterprise-to-Open-Source Decomposition
I modified `docs/enterprise-to-open-source-decomposition.md` to include a new **Phase 6: 2026 High-Profile Trending Projects**, mapping the new roadmap items directly into the existing project architecture.

### 3. Enhanced the Main README
I updated `README.md` to prominently feature the new 2026 Strategic Roadmap and added a new "Build Your Own 2026 Trending App" section under the Builder's Path. This gives immediate visibility to the hottest projects.

### 4. Refined the Contribution Framework
I updated `CONTRIBUTING.md` to explicitly highlight the **🌟 2026 Focus Areas**, encouraging the community to submit projects related to Generative AI, the World Cup, Women's Sports, and Emerging Tech.
